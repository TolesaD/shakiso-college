About Shakiso Poly-Technic College

Shakiso Poly-Technic College is a distinguished governmental institution located in the vibrant Shakiso City Administration. Established with the vision of fostering skilled professionals, the college has been a beacon of technical education in the region since its inception.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, practical, and industry-relevant technical and vocational education and training (TVET) that equips students with the competencies needed to excel in their chosen fields and contribute significantly to national development.

We offer a diverse range of programs across various disciplines, including but not limited to:

  • Automotive Technology
  • Construction and Building Technology
  • Electrical and Electronics Technology
  • Information Technology
  • Manufacturing Technology
  • And many more...

The college boasts state-of-the-art facilities, modern workshops, well-equipped laboratories, and a dedicated team of experienced instructors and faculty members committed to student success. We believe in a hands-on approach to learning, ensuring that our graduates are not just academically proficient but also practically adept and ready for the workforce.

Shakiso Poly-Technic College is more than just an educational institution; it's a community where innovation is nurtured, skills are honed, and future leaders are forged. Join us in shaping a brighter tomorrow!
